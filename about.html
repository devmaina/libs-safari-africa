About Us

At Libs Tours and Travel Agency, we believe travel is about more than just visiting places—it's about immersing yourself in the vibrant cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and unique wildlife that Africa has to offer. From the dunes of the Sahara to the savannahs of Kenya, the rainforests of Congo to the beaches of Zanzibar, we craft journeys that create lifelong memories.
